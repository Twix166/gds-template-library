# Worked example: Discovery findings, options and assessment evidence

This fictional example is for a Discovery into helping people renew a Blue Badge.

## Assumptions log

| ID | Assumption | Why it matters | Risk if wrong | How to test | Owner | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A-001 | Most renewal problems happen because users do not understand timing. | Drives whether clearer reminders are valuable. | Team may improve the wrong part of the service. | Compare call reasons, interview users and review renewal dates. | User Researcher | Testing |
| A-002 | Councils can change reminder wording without policy change. | Affects feasibility of quick improvements. | Alpha idea may need governance or legal approval. | Check with council service owners and policy team. | Product Manager | Open |
| A-003 | Uploading evidence is a major barrier for users with access needs. | Affects inclusion and channel strategy. | Digital route could exclude users. | Observe users with access needs completing evidence tasks. | User Researcher | Open |

## Options assessment

| Option | User value | Feasibility | Cost and effort | Risks | Evidence | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Stop after Discovery | Low | High | Low | Known problems remain unresolved. | Evidence suggests clear user and operational pain. | Do not recommend. |
| Improve reminder and guidance only | Medium | High | Low to medium | May not address evidence upload and status anxiety. | Content review and user interviews show confusion. | Consider as part of Alpha. |
| Prototype end-to-end renewal support | High | Medium | Medium | Requires council and policy alignment. | Research shows linked issues across timing, evidence and status. | Recommend Alpha option. |
| Build renewal tracking immediately | Unknown | Medium | High | Premature technical solution; may duplicate local systems. | Evidence does not yet prove status tracking is the main need. | Do not recommend for immediate build. |

## Discovery findings report outline

### Summary

Renewal problems appear to be caused by a combination of unclear timing, unclear evidence expectations and limited confidence during the waiting period. The team recommends an Alpha to test clearer renewal content, evidence prompts and status communication before committing to build.

### Key findings

| Finding | Evidence | Implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Users often discover renewal action is needed too late. | Interviews and support query themes. | Alpha should test earlier and clearer prompts. |
| Evidence requirements are not understood consistently. | Council staff interviews and rejected evidence samples. | Alpha should test content and evidence examples. |
| Users need reassurance while waiting. | User interviews and contact centre data. | Alpha should test expectation-setting before building tracking. |

### Recommendation

Proceed to Alpha to test whether a clearer renewal journey can reduce avoidable contact and improve confidence for badge holders, carers and caseworkers.
