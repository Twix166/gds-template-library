# Worked example: Alpha evidence pack

This fictional example continues the Blue Badge renewal scenario from Discovery. It shows how Alpha artefacts might fit together when testing clearer renewal guidance, evidence prompts and status communication.

## Alpha plan summary

| Field | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Alpha goal | Test whether clearer content, evidence prompts and expectation-setting can reduce renewal confusion before committing to Beta build. |
| Riskiest assumptions | Users can understand renewal timing from revised prompts; evidence examples reduce incomplete applications; users do not need a full tracker if expectations are clear. |
| Prototype scope | Renewal reminder, eligibility route, evidence guidance, upload prompt and confirmation/status content. |
| Out of scope | Production integration, full caseworker workflow, policy reform, payment or enforcement. |
| Decision at end of Alpha | Proceed to Beta, re-scope, repeat Alpha or stop. |

## Prototype description

| Prototype area | What it tests | Evidence needed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Renewal reminder | Whether users know when to start renewal. | Comprehension testing with badge holders and carers. |
| Evidence guidance | Whether users can identify acceptable evidence. | Usability testing and caseworker review. |
| Confirmation and status content | Whether users understand what happens next without contacting support. | Usability testing and support-risk review. |

## User testing findings

| Finding | Evidence | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Most users understood the renewal window after content changes. | 8 of 10 participants correctly identified when to apply. | Keep revised timing content for Beta. |
| Evidence examples helped carers but not all badge holders. | 4 of 6 badge holders still needed help choosing documents. | Test assisted evidence guidance in Beta. |
| Status expectations reduced anxiety but did not remove all support need. | Participants wanted a clear timescale and contact route. | Beta should include confirmation and support content before building tracking. |

## Alpha recommendation

Proceed to Beta with a limited renewal support journey focused on timing, evidence guidance, confirmation content and assisted digital support. Do not build a full renewal tracker until there is stronger evidence that it is needed.
