# Service Standard Mapping

This mapping shows how templates may support evidence for the GDS Service Standard. It is not a checklist and does not replace the Service Manual or assessment guidance.

| Service Standard area | Likely evidence templates | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Understand users and their needs | `templates/discovery/user-needs-catalogue.md`, `templates/discovery/discovery-findings-report.md`, `templates/alpha/user-research-plan.md`, `templates/alpha/user-testing-findings.md` | Show who users are, what they need, how needs were researched and how findings changed the service. |
| Solve a whole problem for users | `templates/discovery/problem-statement.md`, `templates/discovery/options-assessment.md`, `templates/alpha/mvp-definition.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/requirements-catalogue.md` | Show the problem scope, user outcomes, policy constraints and rationale for the selected approach. |
| Provide a joined-up experience | `templates/discovery/service-blueprint-current-state.md`, `templates/discovery/stakeholder-map.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/dependency-register.md` | Show how the service works across channels, teams, systems and organisations. |
| Make the service simple to use | `templates/discovery/current-state-journey-map.md`, `templates/alpha/prototype-description.md`, `templates/alpha/user-testing-findings.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/business-rules-catalogue.md` | Show journey evidence, usability issues, content or process simplification and iteration. |
| Make sure everyone can use the service | `templates/beta/accessibility-assessment.md`, `templates/alpha/user-research-plan.md`, `templates/alpha/user-testing-findings.md`, `templates/beta/beta-assessment-evidence-pack.md` | Show accessibility planning, testing, issue management and evidence from users with access needs. |
| Have a multidisciplinary team | `templates/discovery/stakeholder-map.md`, `templates/discovery/discovery-assessment-evidence-pack.md`, `templates/alpha/alpha-plan.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/decision-log.md` | Show team roles, governance, collaboration and how decisions involve the right disciplines. |
| Use agile ways of working | `templates/alpha/alpha-plan.md`, `templates/beta/beta-delivery-plan.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/issue-register.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/decision-log.md` | Show iterative planning, prioritisation, regular review and transparent delivery management. |
| Iterate and improve frequently | `templates/discovery/assumptions-log.md`, `templates/alpha/user-testing-findings.md`, `templates/live/continual-improvement-plan.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/traceability-matrix.md` | Show learning loops, validated assumptions, backlog changes and continuous improvement. |
| Create a secure service | `templates/beta/security-risk-assessment.md`, `templates/beta/privacy-assessment.md`, `templates/alpha/non-functional-requirements.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/risk-register.md` | Show security and privacy risks, controls, ownership and residual risk decisions. |
| Define success measures | `templates/discovery/discovery-findings-report.md`, `templates/live/service-dashboard.md`, `templates/live/benefits-realisation-report.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/benefits-register.md` | Show what success means, how it will be measured and what evidence is available. |
| Choose appropriate tools and technology | `templates/alpha/technical-architecture-overview.md`, `templates/alpha/architecture-decision-record.md`, `templates/discovery/options-assessment.md`, `templates/cross-cutting/data-dictionary.md` | Show technology choices, constraints, trade-offs and alignment with service needs. |
| Make new source code open | `templates/alpha/technical-architecture-overview.md`, `templates/alpha/architecture-decision-record.md`, `templates/beta/release-plan.md` | Show open source approach, repository arrangements and exceptions where needed. |
| Use and contribute to open standards | `templates/cross-cutting/data-dictionary.md`, `templates/alpha/technical-architecture-overview.md`, `templates/alpha/architecture-decision-record.md` | Show standards used for data, APIs, interoperability and documentation. |
| Operate a reliable service | `templates/beta/operational-readiness-checklist.md`, `templates/beta/support-model.md`, `templates/live/live-service-management-plan.md`, `templates/live/incident-management-process.md`, `templates/live/operational-runbook.md` | Show support, monitoring, incident response, resilience and live operations arrangements. |
