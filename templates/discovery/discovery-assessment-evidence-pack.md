# Purpose

Bring together Discovery evidence for assessment preparation or internal review.

# When to use this

Use before a Discovery assessment, gateway, assurance review or phase transition decision.

# Inputs

- Discovery findings
- User research evidence
- Team and governance information
- Options and recommendations

# Template

Add evidence summary, links by Service Standard area, known gaps, owners and review actions.

# Quality checklist

- Evidence is linked rather than duplicated.
- Gaps and risks are visible.
- The pack tells a coherent story about what the team learned.

- Discovery findings report
