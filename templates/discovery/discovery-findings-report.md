# Purpose

Summarise Discovery findings, evidence, recommendations and proposed next steps.

# When to use this

Use at the end of Discovery or for major review points.

# Inputs

- Research findings
- Service data
- Technical and operational findings
- Options assessment

# Template

Copy this structure for an end-of-Discovery report or playback document.

## 1. Executive summary

Summarise the problem, what the team learned, and the recommended decision.

# Quality checklist

- Recommendations are traceable to evidence.
- Remaining uncertainty is clear.
- The report supports a decision about what to do next.
