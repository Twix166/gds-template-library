# Purpose

Track assumptions, evidence gaps, validation actions and decisions.

# When to use this

Use from Discovery onwards whenever the team is making decisions under uncertainty.

# Inputs

- Team workshops
- Research questions
- Technical spikes
- Policy or operational unknowns

# Template

Add assumption, area, impact, confidence, validation method, owner, due date and outcome.

# Quality checklist

- High-impact assumptions have validation actions.
- Owners and dates are clear.
- Validated assumptions are closed or converted into decisions.
