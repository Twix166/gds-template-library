# Purpose

Identify stakeholders, their interests, influence, responsibilities and engagement needs.

# When to use this

Use early in Discovery and maintain as the service context becomes clearer.

# Inputs

- Organisation charts
- Policy and operational contacts
- Supplier and partner information
- Governance forums

# Template

Add stakeholder names or groups, role, interest, influence, decision rights, contact route and engagement approach.

# Quality checklist

- Key decision makers and operational stakeholders are included.
- External organisations and affected teams are visible.
- Engagement actions have owners.
