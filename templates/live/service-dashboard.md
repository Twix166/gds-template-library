- Analytics
- Operational data
- Support data

- Data definitions are clear.

# Field glossary

Hover over table headers, and some field-name cells, to see short definitions in renderers that support HTML title text.

- **Action threshold**: The point at which a metric should trigger review or intervention.
- **Audience**: The people or groups who will use the dashboard or report.
- **Baseline**: The current or starting value used to compare future change.
- **Current result**: The most recent measured value.
- **Dashboard owner**: The person accountable for maintaining and interpreting the dashboard.
- **Data source**: Where the data comes from.
- **Data sources**: The datasets, systems or reports used.
- **Decision or action**: The decision made, or the action agreed.
- **Definition**: A precise explanation of what the measure or severity means.
- **Due date**: The date by which an action or condition should be completed.
- **Field**: The name of the information item to complete.
- **Measure**: A metric or indicator used to judge performance or success.
- **Owner**: The person or role accountable for the item.
- **Reporting cadence**: How often reporting happens.
- **Response**: The content to complete for the field.
- **Service name**: The name of the service being documented.
- **Target**: The intended value or threshold for a measure.
- **Trend**: Whether the measure is improving, stable or declining.
- **What the data suggests**: The team interpretation of the metric or evidence.
