# Purpose

Review live service performance, risks, issues, user feedback and improvement priorities.

# When to use this

Use at regular service governance meetings or major review points.

# Inputs

- Service dashboard
- Incident reports
- User research or feedback
- Risk and issue registers

# Quality checklist

- Review uses current evidence.
- Decisions and actions have owners.
- Improvement priorities are linked to user and service outcomes.

# Field glossary

Hover over table headers, and some field-name cells, to see short definitions in renderers that support HTML title text.

- **Action**: The specific follow-up task needed to progress, validate or close an item.
- **Area**: The part of the service, organisation or delivery context this row relates to.
- **Attendees**: People or roles expected to attend the review.
- **Chair**: The person responsible for running the review.
- **Decision or action**: The decision made, or the action agreed.
- **Due date**: The date by which an action or condition should be completed.
- **Evidence**: The research, data, observation or source that supports the statement.
- **Field**: The name of the information item to complete.
- **Interpretation**: What the team thinks the evidence or data means.
- **Owner**: The person or role accountable for the item.
- **Period covered**: The time period covered by a review or report.
- **Reason**: Why the item is included, excluded, changed or recommended.
- **Response**: The content to complete for the field.
- **Review date**: The date an item should be reviewed.
- **Service name**: The name of the service being documented.
- **Status**: The current state of the item.
