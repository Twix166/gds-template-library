# Purpose

Define how the live service will be managed, monitored, supported and improved.

# When to use this

Use when the service enters Live and review at regular service governance points.

# Inputs

- Support model
- Operational runbook
- Service dashboard
- Governance requirements

# Template

Add service scope, roles, governance, monitoring, support, reporting, risks and improvement approach.

# Quality checklist

- Live responsibilities are clear.
- Service health measures are defined.
- Improvement and governance cycles are documented.
