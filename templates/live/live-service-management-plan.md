# Purpose

Define how the live service will be managed, monitored, supported and improved.

# When to use this

Use when the service enters Live and review at regular service governance points.

# Inputs

- Support model
- Operational runbook
- Service dashboard
- Governance requirements

- Service health measures are defined.
- Improvement and governance cycles are documented.

# Field glossary

Hover over table headers, and some field-name cells, to see short definitions in renderers that support HTML title text.

- **Action**: The specific follow-up task needed to progress, validate or close an item.
- **Approach**: The planned method or way of working for this area.
- **Area**: The part of the service, organisation or delivery context this row relates to.
- **Cadence**: How often the activity, review or communication happens.
- **Dashboard link**: A link to the service dashboard or performance report.
- **Evidence**: The research, data, observation or source that supports the statement.
- **Evidence or output**: The output produced or evidence recorded by the activity.
- **Field**: The name of the information item to complete.
- **Item**: The risk, improvement, decision or issue being tracked.
- **Live date**: The date the service entered or is expected to enter Live.
- **Owner**: The person or role accountable for the item.
- **Response**: The content to complete for the field.
- **Responsibility**: What the role, team or component is responsible for.
- **Review cadence**: How often the item is reviewed.
- **Role or forum**: A governance forum, role or group involved in managing the service.
- **Service name**: The name of the service being documented.
- **Service owner**: The person accountable for the service.
- **Status**: The current state of the item.
- **Support model link**: A link to the support model for the live service.
