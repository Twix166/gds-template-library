# Purpose

Provide operational procedures for common support, maintenance and recovery tasks.

# When to use this

Use when the service is moving into Live operations or when operational procedures change.

# Inputs

- Architecture overview
- Support model
- Incident management process
- Monitoring and alerting information

# Template

Add procedure, trigger, steps, expected result, escalation, rollback and evidence of completion.

# Quality checklist

- Steps are clear enough for the intended support role.
- Risks and escalation points are explicit.
- Procedures are tested or reviewed.
