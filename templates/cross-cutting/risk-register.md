# Purpose

Track risks, causes, impacts, mitigations, owners and review dates.

# When to use this

Use throughout delivery and Live operation.

# Inputs

- Team risk reviews
- Security and privacy assessments
- Delivery plans
- Operational reviews

# Template

Add risk ID, description, cause, impact, likelihood, severity, mitigation, owner, status and review date.

# Quality checklist

- Risks describe uncertain future events, not current issues.
- Mitigations are actionable and owned.
- High risks are reviewed regularly.
