# Purpose

Track internal and external dependencies that affect delivery or service operation.

# When to use this

Use when the service depends on other teams, systems, suppliers, data, policy or governance decisions.

# Inputs

- Delivery plans
- Stakeholder map
- Architecture overview
- Governance requirements

# Template

Add dependency ID, description, provider, consumer, impact, date needed, owner, status and escalation route.

# Quality checklist

- Dependencies are specific and time-bound.
- Owners exist on both sides where possible.
- Blocked dependencies are escalated.
