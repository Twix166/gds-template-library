# Purpose

Track active issues that need action, escalation or decision.

# When to use this

Use when delivery, design, technical or governance issues need transparent management.

# Inputs

- Delivery stand-ups
- Retrospectives
- Assurance reviews
- Support or operational reports

# Template

Add issue ID, description, impact, owner, action, due date, escalation route, status and decision needed.

# Quality checklist

- Issues are current and actionable.
- Owners and due dates are clear.
- Escalations are recorded where needed.
