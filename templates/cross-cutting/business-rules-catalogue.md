# Purpose

Record business rules, their sources, owners, rationale and impact on users or operations.

# When to use this

Use when policy, operational or eligibility rules shape the service.

# Inputs

- Policy documents
- Operational guidance
- Subject matter expert input
- Existing process rules

# Template

Add rule ID, rule statement, source, owner, rationale, affected journey, exceptions and status.

# Quality checklist

- Rules are written plainly.
- Sources and owners are recorded.
- Unnecessary complexity or exceptions are challenged.
