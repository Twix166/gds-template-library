# Purpose

Plan Beta delivery, releases, dependencies, governance and team ways of working.

# When to use this

Use at the start of Beta and keep updated through delivery planning.

# Inputs

- Alpha recommendations
- MVP definition
- Team capacity
- Risks and dependencies

# Template

Copy this structure to plan build, release, testing, assurance and live readiness work.

# Quality checklist

- Dependencies and assumptions are visible.
- Plan updates are based on evidence from delivery.

# Field glossary

Hover over table headers, and some field-name cells, to see short definitions in renderers that support HTML title text.

- **Attendees**: People or roles expected to attend the review.
- **Beta goal**: The main delivery and learning outcome the Beta is intended to achieve.
- **Date or sprint**: The planned date, sprint or delivery timebox.
- **Dependencies**: Other teams, systems, suppliers, decisions or events the work depends on.
- **Evidence required**: The evidence needed for the milestone or decision.
- **Field**: The name of the information item to complete.
- **Forum or ceremony**: A regular meeting, governance forum or agile ceremony.
- **Frequency**: How often the item is reviewed or reported.
- **Lead**: The person leading the workstream.
- **Live transition criteria**: The conditions that must be met before moving to Live.
- **Milestone**: A significant delivery, release or governance point.
- **Outcome**: The result or change the team wants to achieve.
- **Outputs**: The evidence, artefacts or decisions produced by the forum.
- **Owner**: The person or role accountable for the item.
- **Private beta scope**: The features, users and service areas included in private beta.
- **Public beta scope**: The features, users and service areas included in public beta.
- **Purpose**: Why the activity, test or artefact exists.
- **Response**: The content to complete for the field.
- **Risks**: Uncertain events or conditions that could affect outcomes.
- **Scope**: The boundaries of what is included.
- **Service name**: The name of the service being documented.
- **Workstream**: A delivery area with a defined scope and lead.
