# Purpose

Bring together Beta evidence for assessment preparation or internal review.

# When to use this

Use before a Beta assessment, live readiness review or public launch decision.

# Inputs

- Beta delivery evidence
- Research and accessibility evidence
- Security and privacy evidence
- Operational readiness evidence

# Template

Add evidence summary, links by Service Standard area, open issues, risks, decisions and actions.

# Quality checklist

- Evidence covers design, build, assurance and operations.
- Open issues have owners and dates.
- The pack supports a clear decision about live readiness.
