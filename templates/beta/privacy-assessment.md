# Purpose

Assess personal data use, privacy risks, lawful basis, mitigations and evidence.

# When to use this

Use when the service processes personal data or changes how personal data is handled.

# Inputs

- Data dictionary
- Service blueprint
- Security risk assessment
- Information governance advice

# Template

Use this structure alongside formal DPIA or information governance processes. Do not replace required legal review.

# Quality checklist

- Personal data use is necessary and proportionate.
- Risks and mitigations are owned.
- Links to formal DPIA or privacy notices are included where relevant.

# Field glossary

Hover over table headers, and some field-name cells, to see short definitions in renderers that support HTML title text.

- **DPIA link**: A link to the Data Protection Impact Assessment, where one exists.
- **Data item**: A specific piece or category of data.
- **Date**: The date the item was recorded or changed.
- **Decision or approval**: A privacy, security, governance or delivery approval.
- **Evidence link**: A link to the evidence supporting the entry.
- **Field**: The name of the information item to complete.
- **Impact**: The effect on users, operations, policy, delivery or technology.
- **Information asset owner**: The person accountable for an information asset.
- **Lawful basis**: The lawful basis relied on for processing personal data.
- **Likelihood**: How likely the risk is to occur.
- **Mitigation**: The action or control used to reduce impact or likelihood.
- **Owner**: The person or role accountable for the item.
- **Personal data involved**: The personal data collected, stored, shared or otherwise processed.
- **Privacy notice link**: A link to the privacy information shown to users.
- **Purpose**: Why the activity, test or artefact exists.
- **Recipient**: The person, team, system or organisation receiving the data.
- **Residual risk**: The level of risk remaining after controls or mitigations are applied.
- **Response**: The content to complete for the field.
- **Retention**: How long the data is kept and when it is deleted or reviewed.
- **Risk**: An uncertain event or condition that could affect outcomes.
- **Sensitivity**: The sensitivity or handling category of the data.
- **Service area**: The part of the service covered by the artefact.
- **Source**: Where the data or evidence comes from.
