# Purpose

Check that the service, team, support model and operational controls are ready for release.

# When to use this

Use before private beta, public beta, live launch or significant operational changes.

# Inputs

- Release plan
- Support model
- Incident management process
- Operational runbook

# Template

Add readiness area, criteria, evidence, owner, status, risks and go-live decision.

# Quality checklist

- Support, monitoring and incident response are covered.
- Open risks are understood by decision makers.
- Evidence is current and linked.
