# Government Digital Service Template Library

This repository is a reusable, open-source template toolkit for UK government digital delivery teams.

It provides starting points for common delivery outputs and evidence artefacts used across the Discovery, Alpha, Beta and Live service lifecycle. The templates are designed to help teams work in line with the GDS Service Manual, Service Standard and good practice for user-centred, agile service delivery.

## Who this is for

The library is intended for multidisciplinary teams working in or around UK government digital services, including:

- Business Analysts
- Service Designers
- User Researchers
- Product Managers
- Delivery Managers
- Technical Architects
- Security, privacy and accessibility specialists
- Operations and governance teams
- Assessment preparation leads

## Alignment with GDS delivery phases

Templates are grouped around the main GDS delivery phases:

- **Discovery**: understand the problem, users, context, constraints and options.
- **Alpha**: test hypotheses, prototype solutions and explore technical and operational approaches.
- **Beta**: build, test and prepare the service for public or private use.
- **Live**: operate, measure, improve and support the service.

The library also includes cross-cutting templates that are useful throughout delivery, such as risks, decisions, requirements, dependencies and benefits.

## How templates are organised

Templates are organised in three complementary ways:

- `templates/` contains the main reusable Markdown templates, grouped by lifecycle phase and cross-cutting use.
- `lifecycle/` provides phase-based space for future guidance, checklists and example packs.
- `domains/` provides domain-based space for future guidance by discipline, such as business analysis, service design, architecture, privacy and operations.

Use `template-index.md` to find templates by phase, domain and purpose. Use `service-standard-mapping.md` to understand how templates may support evidence for Service Standard assessment.

## How to use the templates

Start with [GDS Templates 101](docs/gds-templates-101.md) for a short walkthrough of when documents are usually started across Discovery, Alpha, Beta and Live.

1. Find the template that best matches the artefact you need.
2. Copy it into your service team workspace or repository.
3. Complete only the sections that are useful for your context.
4. Link to supporting evidence rather than duplicating large documents.
5. Review the quality checklist before sharing or using the artefact for assessment preparation.

## Important note

These templates are starting points, not mandatory GDS formats.

They are intended to help teams produce clear, consistent and useful delivery evidence. Teams should adapt the structure, language and level of detail to their service context, user needs, delivery phase, organisational governance and assessment requirements.

The Service Manual and Service Standard should remain the primary sources of guidance.
