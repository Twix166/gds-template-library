# Live

Live is where the service is operated, measured, supported and improved. Use Live artefacts to show that the service remains reliable, accessible, secure and responsive to user needs.

## Recommended artefacts

| Stage of Live | Artefact | What it is used for | How to use it | Referenced in GDS guidance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Live setup | [Live service management plan](../../templates/live/live-service-management-plan.md) | Define how the service is managed, governed, monitored and improved. | Review it whenever ownership, support or governance changes. | [Service Standard point 14](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-14-operate-a-reliable-service); [Live phase](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/agile-delivery/how-the-live-phase-works). |
| Performance | [Service dashboard](../../templates/live/service-dashboard.md) | Define live measures, data sources and reporting cadence. | Use measures that support decisions and continuous improvement. | [Service Standard point 10](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-10-define-success-publish-performance-data); [Measuring success](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/measuring-success). |
| Incidents | [Incident management process](../../templates/live/incident-management-process.md) | Define incident detection, response, communication and review. | Test it and update it after incidents or exercises. | [Service Standard point 14](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-14-operate-a-reliable-service). |
| Operations | [Operational runbook](../../templates/live/operational-runbook.md) | Provide operational procedures for support and maintenance. | Keep procedures short, tested and clear enough for the intended support role. | [Service Standard point 14](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-14-operate-a-reliable-service). |
| Governance | [Service review](../../templates/live/service-review.md) | Review performance, risks, issues, feedback and decisions. | Use regularly with current data and clear action ownership. | [Service Standard point 8](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-8-iterate-and-improve-frequently); [Service Standard point 10](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-10-define-success-publish-performance-data). |
| Improvement | [Continual improvement plan](../../templates/live/continual-improvement-plan.md) | Plan evidence-based improvements after launch. | Prioritise improvements by user value, service risk and effort. | [Service Standard point 8](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-8-iterate-and-improve-frequently). |
| Benefits | [Benefits realisation report](../../templates/live/benefits-realisation-report.md) | Track realised benefits against expected outcomes. | Compare results with baselines and explain variance. | [Service Standard point 10](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard/point-10-define-success-publish-performance-data). |
| Assessment preparation | [Live assessment evidence pack](../../templates/live/live-assessment-evidence-pack.md) | Bring together Live evidence for assessment or assurance review. | Show how the service operates and improves in practice. | [Service assessments](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-assessments); [Service Standard](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard). |

## Suggested sequence

1. Confirm live ownership, support and governance.
2. Maintain a service dashboard and review it regularly.
3. Keep incident, runbook and support information current.
4. Use service reviews to make decisions and update the improvement plan.
5. Track benefits against baselines and expected outcomes.
6. Build assessment evidence from routine live management, not one-off documents.
